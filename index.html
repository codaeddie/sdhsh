My name is Nada Benny with Realty Executives Dillon. I have been a full time Realtor in San Diego since 2013 and it's safe to say I nerd out on all things real estate related! The majority of my business over the past 12 years is from referrals and repeat clients. This means the world to me.

I believe knowledge is power and I love empowering my clients with my expertise and experience to accomplish their real estate goals. Selling and buying a home can be challenging when you don't have an advocate on your side... Sell and buy with me and allow me to carry the weight off your shoulders!

When you buy with me, you can expect a strategic approach that ensures you are able to attain the American dream of homeownership without throwing any logic out the window. Home buying with peace of mind while keeping your deposit protected is my guarantee.
